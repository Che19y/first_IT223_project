Daily Log : Day 231

We are exploring the outer rim of the space. The mission to Alpha Centauri is a deep space expidition designed to explore distant star systems and
search for potentially habitable planets. The crew aboard the USS Observer will travel for many years through space, collecting data and scanning unknown worlds.
Because of the importance of this journey, all mission information is marked classified and closely monitored inside by the Graugentaur ship.

Before leaving Earth, the crew must prepare and bring essential supplies such as food, water, oxygen tanks, medical kits, and scientific instruments.
Advanced navigation systems and communication equipment are also installed to ensure the ship can safely reach its destination.
Every part of the ship is carefully checked to protect the crew and guarantee the success of the mission
